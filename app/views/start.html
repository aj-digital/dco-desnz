Track your Development Consent Order (DCO) case

Use this service to view the current status of your DCO case with the Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ) and make payment if requested.

This service is for applicants whose DCO case has been referred to DESNZ for a decision. You do not use this service to submit your DCO application — that is done through the statutory process via the Planning Inspectorate (PINS).

What you can do

  • view the current status of your DCO case
  • check the statutory decision deadline
  • make a one-off payment if one has been requested

Before you start

You will need:

  • the email address associated with your DCO case
  • access to your GOV.UK One Login account

If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login, you will be able to create one.

How the process works

  1. Your DCO application is examined by the Planning Inspectorate (PINS)
  2. PINS sends its recommendation to DESNZ
  3. DESNZ reviews the case and makes a decision
  4. You can track progress and make payment here
  5. DESNZ issues its decision
